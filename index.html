H.manager

A human resource (HR) manager is a professional responsible for managing the various aspects of an organization's human resources department. Their primary role is to oversee the recruitment, selection, training, and development of employees. HR managers also handle employee relations, compensation and benefits, performance management, and ensure compliance with employment laws and regulations.
